# CTS-Protocol

**give-back, visible, verifiable.**

---

## What This Is

This repo is the public infrastructure behind **#CTS** — a responsibility layer for monetized influence.

Not a campaign. Not a charity drive. A mechanism.

If your income comes from people's attention —
their time, their trust, their clicks —
this asks one question:

*Do you owe them anything back?*

Not legally. Not contractually.

Ethically.

---

## The Problem

The attention economy extracts motivation and sells dreams.  
Money flows up. Almost nothing flows back.  
Influence without accountability is the largest unregulated power system in human history.

Platforms won't fix this.  
Regulations are too slow.  

So a new layer can emerge — built by people, not imposed.

---

## The Mechanism

**#CTS — Challenge To Share**

If you earn from influence:
- Give back 1% of net income monthly, OR
- 10% of a specific revenue stream

Make it visible. Post proof. Use `#CTS @yourname`.

Amount matters less than the act of making it visible.  
Because visible behavior, repeated at scale, changes norms.

This repo is the public record of that standard.
